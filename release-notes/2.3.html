WindowSnap 2.3

  • Dictate Anywhere — press the shortcut, speak, and your words are transcribed and pasted into any app (uses the same on-device Whisper model as translation).
  • Window Switcher — an Alt-Tab-style overlay of all open windows with thumbnails; Tab/arrows to navigate, Return to switch.
  • Meeting bar — your next calendar meeting in the menu, with a one-click Join for Zoom/Meet/Teams/Webex.
  • Keystroke visualizer — show pressed keys on screen for demos and recordings.
  • New Convert tab — live currency conversion (with country, continent, inverse rate, adjustable decimals, pin/hide/reorder), a worldtimebuddy-style World Time comparer with a 5-minute slider and one-click calendar events, plus length, mass, temperature, volume, data, speed, time, area and angle.
  • Command Palette calculator — type math, unit or currency conversions and copy the result.
  • Tidied the menu bar and settings tabs.
