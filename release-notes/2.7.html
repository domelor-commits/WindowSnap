WindowSnap 2.7

  • Choose where calendar events go — the "＋ Calendar Event" dialog now lets you pick the destination: Apple Calendar, the Outlook app, or Outlook on the web. Your last choice is remembered.
  • Works with New Outlook — events can be added to Outlook on the web (via a pre-filled composer), which works even with New Outlook for Mac. The "Outlook app" option appears only when classic Outlook is running, since New Outlook can't be automated.
  • Outlook events land in your real calendar — when saving to the Outlook app, events now go to your synced account calendar instead of the local "On My Computer" store.
